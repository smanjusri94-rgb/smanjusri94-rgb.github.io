About Me

I’m Manjusri Sridhara, a Master’s student at Arizona State University specializing in analog and RF integrated circuit design. I have strong transistor-level experience in high-gain amplifiers, biasing networks, and low-power analog blocks.

My work emphasizes stability, noise, linearity, and power efficiency, validated through rigorous Cadence-based simulations. I also design RF/mmWave front-end circuits (20–24 GHz), including LNAs and mixers.

My goal is to translate theory into robust, silicon-ready IC solutions.
